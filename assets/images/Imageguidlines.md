**Important Note:-**

* Photo size must be in between 100KB to 200KB.
* Image resolution must be 640 x 480 or better.
* Photo must be in .jpg file format.
* Photos with sun-glasses are not acceptable, as eyes must be clearly visible.
* Half-body photos and full-body photos are not acceptable, as facial features are the focus point for Facial Recognition.
* Black & White photos are not acceptable.
* Facial Features must be taken at full frontal view.
* Please do not use an image from a photo.
* Photos with cap and facemask are not acceptable.

**Please comply to the FR photo guideline.**
